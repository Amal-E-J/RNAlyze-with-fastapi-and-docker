RNAlyse is a webapp that lets you find if a given RNAs are coding or not. It is free, easy to use and fast. No data is saved. RNAlyse can help biologists identify whether their RNA sequences are coding or not. This will help them understand the function of the gene they are studying and how their cDNAs are working together with other genes in order to produce proteins.
